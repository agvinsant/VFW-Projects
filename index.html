This app is for anyone who tends to forget what they are supposed to do. Your list is stored locally on your device so as of right now, you cannot access your list from multiple devices. Use this app from your browser on any smartphone, tablet PC, or your home PC or laptop.

This app is a project for the Visual Frameworks class at Full Sail University. This app is and will always be free to use. Enjoy, hope it makes your life easier! -- Adam Vinsant
